Overview

1st Studio is a modern beauty salon in Dubai offering premium hair, skin and nail care services. The main task was to create a website that would emphasize the elegance of the brand, offer easy navigation and inspire customers to sign up for services. I focused on a design that combines luxurious minimalism and convenience, with an emphasis on visual elements that emphasize professionalism and high-quality salon services.

Results

The 1st Studio website has received many positive reviews due to its aesthetics and ease of use. After the launch, the number of online entries increased by 30%, and customers noted the intuitive interface that makes the booking process fast and enjoyable
